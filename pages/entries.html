Everything Else

Caption: "The brain sculpture resembles the brain and the complexity of its inherent brain activity patterns. From a global point of view, a from moment-to-moment varying set of diverse regions is simultaneously active, partly engaging in different processes. Researchers aim to disentangle these processes to better understand their relationships which ultimately is assumed to provide insights about the functioning of the brain. The diversity of processes and regions is reflected by the different forms and colours of lights. Entangled wires demonstrate the complexity of connectivity patterns in the brain. (I made this all up after finishing the brain sculpture. My main aim was to put colourful lights into a brain.)"
